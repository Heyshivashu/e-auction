Vehicle

Government vehicle auctions typically sell a wide range of vehicles that have been seized, surplus, or are no longer in use by government agencies. The types of vehicles you might find at a government auction can include: Used Government Vehicles,Seized Vehicles,Surplus Military Vehicles ,Fleet vehicles, Confiscated Vehicles, These auctions can offer a variety of vehicles in different conditions and price ranges. Some may require repairs or maintenance, while others might be in relatively good condition.
